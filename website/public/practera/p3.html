1 · Purpose & Vision

Diagnose in Real Time

Evidence Growth

2 · Capturing Off‑System Work

AI Meeting Capture

Optional bot joins Zoom/Teams calls, transcribes, extracts decisions & blockers, and logs them as xAPI events. Summaries reach the Issue Queue automatically.

Doc & Code Watchers

GitHub, Google Drive, and SharePoint adapters parse commits, comments, and edits—flagging scope creep or missing attribution and nudging teams accordingly.

Daily Reflection Nudges

Micro‑prompts meet students where they work (Slack, email) to capture quick insights—structured instantly for analytics.

3 · AI Project Scaffolding

Project Brief Builder

Conversational wizard turns vague ideas into SMART goals aligned with rubrics and timelines.

Task‑Decomposition AI

Auto‑creates Kanban tasks, owners, and due dates—syncing to calendars and dashboards.

Adaptive Checkpoints

When progress slows, the system inserts extra milestones or learning objects to unblock momentum.

4 · Skills‑as‑Trajectory Feedback

What We Measure

Baseline + weekly delta on ten professional skills, sentiment from transcripts, expert feedback, and MR scores. Velocity Score tracks rate of improvement, not just current level.

Student Dashboard Widgets

“Weakest Skill” spotlights, actionable micro‑tasks, velocity sparklines, and before/after evidence clips boost self‑regulated learning.

Educator Heat‑Maps

Cohort view shows who’s accelerating or plateauing, enabling targeted coaching interventions.

5 · Mixed‑Reality Ready

xAPI Adapters

Unity & Unreal plug‑ins stream verbs like practiced and demonstrated with scenario metadata straight into Practera.

Skill Mapping

Each MR scenario tags the specific rubric rows it trains, so gains count instantly toward velocity metrics.

Cross‑Modal Dashboards

Combine MR scores with expert ratings and meeting sentiment for a holistic view of learner growth.
